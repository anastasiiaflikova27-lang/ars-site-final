# ÁRS Interior Design — Website for an Investment Renovation Studio

A commercial multi-section website for **ÁRS Interior Design**, a Saint Petersburg based interior and renovation studio focused on investment-oriented apartment renovations, home staging, design projects and full-cycle property preparation for sale or rent.

The website was created as a portfolio-level client project with a strong focus on visual presentation, clear service structure, trust-building content and conversion-oriented user flow.

## Live Website

https://ars-site-final.vercel.app

## Project Overview

ÁRS Interior Design works with apartments that are prepared for sale, rent or personal living. The main idea of the website was to present renovation not only as a visual design service, but as a business-oriented process that can increase the value and attractiveness of a property.

The website communicates the studio’s full-cycle approach: from property analysis and concept development to renovation, furnishing, home staging and final preparation for sale or rent.

## Project Goal

The goal of this project was to create a modern, elegant and trustworthy website that would:

- Present the studio as a serious and reliable partner for property owners and investors
- Explain the difference between service formats in a clear and structured way
- Show real project cases with measurable results
- Make the user understand how the renovation process works
- Help potential clients quickly contact the studio through Telegram, WhatsApp, Instagram or phone
- Create a premium but clean visual identity suitable for the real estate and interior design niche

## Design Direction

The visual concept was built around a clean editorial layout with a premium real estate feeling. The website needed to look professional, calm and expensive without becoming overloaded or too decorative.

The design direction includes:

- Minimalistic structure
- Large typography
- Clean spacing
- Elegant neutral color palette
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Case-based storytelling
- Clear service cards
- Simple and intuitive navigation
- Conversion-focused call-to-action blocks

The final style supports the positioning of the studio as a team that works not only with beauty and aesthetics, but also with investment logic, property value and practical results.

## Website Structure

The website includes the following main sections:

### 1. Hero Section

The first screen introduces the brand and communicates the main positioning:

> Renovation that works for your capital.

The hero section quickly explains that the studio turns apartments into assets through renovation, design, furnishing and preparation for sale or rent.

### 2. About the Studio

This section explains the full-cycle approach of the studio. It shows that the client does not need to manage the renovation process daily, because the studio takes responsibility for the key stages.

The section highlights the involvement of:

- Designers
- Visualizers
- Construction teams
- Realtor support

### 3. Services

The services are divided into clear formats:

- Investment renovation
- Design project
- Furnishing and home staging
- Renovation for personal living

Each service card explains who the format is for, what is included and what result the client can expect.

### 4. Property Selection Support

This section was added to show that the studio can also help with choosing a property before renovation. This is especially important for clients who buy apartments as investment objects.

The section explains that the team can help evaluate the potential of an apartment before renovation and connect the process of purchase, renovation, furnishing, sale or rent.

### 5. Realized Projects

The project section presents real case studies with measurable results. The goal was to make the studio’s work more concrete and trustworthy.

Examples of projects shown on the website include:

- Kavalergardskaya
- ID Park Pobedy
- Glorax Baltiyskaya
- Lenina
- ЖК Kvadria
- ЖК Poklonnaya 9

Each case is positioned not just as a visual portfolio item, but as a business result: increased property value, rental income or preparation for sale.

### 6. Why Work With Us

This section explains the studio’s key advantages:

- Full-cycle process
- Project control
- Team coordination
- Clear result
- Less stress for the client
- Apartment ready for sale, rent or living

### 7. Work Process

The process section breaks the work into clear stages:

1. Property and task analysis
2. Concept
3. Design project
4. Renovation implementation
5. Furnishing
6. Property handover
7. Sale or rental support

This helps users understand what happens after the first contact and reduces uncertainty before reaching out.

### 8. Pricing

The pricing section presents transparent service packages for different apartment sizes and formats.

The website includes pricing for:

- Investment renovation
- Design project
- Furnishing and home staging

The goal of this section was to make the offer more concrete and easier to compare.

### 9. Contact Section

The contact section gives the user several simple ways to start communication:

- Telegram
- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- Phone
- Email

The call-to-action is intentionally simple: the user can describe their apartment, its condition and goal, and the studio can suggest the right format.

## Challenges

One of the main challenges was turning a complex service into a simple and understandable website structure.

The studio offers several related services: design, renovation, furnishing, home staging, property selection and sale/rent preparation. The task was to organize these directions so that users could quickly understand:

- What the studio does
- Which service format suits them
- What result they can expect
- How the process works
- How much the basic packages cost
- How to contact the studio

Another important challenge was presenting the projects not only as interior photos, but as business cases with measurable value.

The final result is a modern, structured and conversion-focused website that presents ÁRS Interior Design as a reliable studio for investment renovations and full-cycle apartment preparation.
